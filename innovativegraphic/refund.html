Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: June 10, 2023

Digital Product Policy

All sales of digital graphic bundles are final. Due to the nature of digital products that can be duplicated, we do not offer refunds or exchanges once a purchase is completed.

Before purchasing, please carefully review the product descriptions, previews, and system requirements.

Exceptions

In rare cases, refunds may be considered under these circumstances:

  • Duplicate purchase: If you accidentally purchased the same product twice
  • Technical issues: If the files are corrupted and we cannot provide working alternatives
  • Misrepresentation: If the product received significantly differs from the advertised product

Refund Process

  1. Email us at support@innovativegraphic.com within 48 hours of purchase
  2. Include your order number and detailed reason for the refund request
  3. Our team will review your request within 3 business days
  4. If approved, refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 5-10 business days

Order Cancellations

You may cancel your order only if:

  • The order hasn't been processed yet (typically within 1 hour of purchase)
  • You haven't downloaded any files from the order

To request cancellation, immediately contact us at support@innovativegraphic.com with your order number.

Contact Us

For any questions about our refund policy, please contact our support team:

  • Email: support@innovativegraphic.com
  • Response Time: Within 24-48 business hours
